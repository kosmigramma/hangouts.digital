Virtual Rooms

Kosmi revolves around a virtual room system. You can think of virtual rooms as extensively featured chat rooms. Features include:

  • In-room chat
  • Webcam/Microphone support
  • Instant invite using a URL
  • Ability to run Real-time applications such as games, emulators and synced video
Applications▼

NES Party and SNES Party

Start a NES or SNES Emulator in the room, load a ROM and enjoy instant multiplayer with room members!

Texas Holdem Poker

Your own private Texas Holdem Poker table to play with your buddies in the room!

It has webcam support so players faces appear right in their seats, making the experience feel almost as if you are playing in real life!

OpenArena

OpenArena is a free clone of the famous FPS Quake III Arena. Start a server in the room and enjoy this classic FPS with room members!

Virtual Cardtable

Start a Virtual Cardtable in the room.

Play any cardgame that can be played on a standard deck of cards.

It has webcam support so players faces appear right in their seats, making the experience feel almost as if you are playing in real life!

Cowatch

Load your own local video files into the room.

The video gets synced and streamed to other members so you can watch together!

YouTube

Watch YouTube videos with the members of the room!

The Video is synced with everyone so you can cowatch YouTube videos at will. Just put in a YouTube URL and click Play!

  • List of public rooms to join
