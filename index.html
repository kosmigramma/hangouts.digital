Applications

SNES Party
SNES Party is an in-browser online emulator for the SNES where you can play SNES games in online multiplayer with your buddies, simply by sharing a link!
snes.party
NES Party
In the same way as SNES Party, NES Party is an in-browser online emulator for the NES where you can play NES games in online multiplayer with your buddies, simply by sharing a link!
nes.party
OpenArena Live
OpenArena is a free clone of the famous FPS Quake III Arena. Unafilliated with the OpenArena team, this project allows playing the game completely within the browser. The servers are run by the users using p2p technology powered by WebRTC. This means anyone can run a gameserver from the browser using a simple UI!
openarena.live
Kosmi Poker
Kosmi Poker a way to create a private Poker room to play with your buddies! Using WebRTC you can even communicate using webcam/microphone with members of the room and the members faces appear in their seats, making the experience feel just as if you are playing in real life!
poker.kosmi.io
Cardtables Online
Cardtables Online is a way to play any cardgame with your friends using a virtual cardtable. You simply create a room with a shareable link and play any card game with your buddies. You can even communicate using webcam/microphone with members of the table.
cardtables.online
CoWatch Video
Ever wanted to watch videos with your friends but you're not in the same room? Now you can! Just load your video and share a link. You can even chat with your friends with a microphone while watching!
cowatch.video
